Step by step: How to download Facebook Messenger Chat

Follow these steps to get a copy of your Facebook Messenger Chat

Step 1

Login to your Facbook account and click on a triangle in the right-top corner of your screen (1). Choose your Settings (2) and next, click on Your Facebook Information (3)

Step 2

On this page you can select data you want to download from Facebook. You want to download only Facebook Messenger content so Deselect All and than mark Messages box (1). Mark type of file you want to download, choose JSON type and clik Create File(3).

Step 3

Once your chats are ready (this process can take until 24h) you get notification (3) on your Facebook Page and you click on Available Copies and you can download (3) your Facebook Messenger Chat

Step 4

When you have downloaded your facebook chat archive. You have to unzip it using right mouse button an choosing Extract Here. After extracting process you will get folder named messages.

Step 5

When you open messages folder you will see most likely 5 folders. One of them is going to be inbox. In inbox folder you will have all messages organise in folders. You can see two kinds of folders. One kind with name contains only small letters shows witch is shown with green arrows and this is where required file is stored. In the folder where name contain capital letter marked with red arrows you can find pictures, gifs used in that conversation.

Step 6

Inside folder where name contain only small letters you will find a file named message_1.json or if you have long conversation it may be more files with numbers. This is your message file
