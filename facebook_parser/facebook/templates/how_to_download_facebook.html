Step by step: How to download Facebook Messenger Chat

Follow these steps to get a copy of your Facebook Messenger Chat

Step 1

Login to your Facbook account and click on a triangle in the right-top corner of your screen (1). Choose your Settings (2) and next, click on Your Facebook Information (3)

Step 2

On this page you can select data you want to download from Facebook. You want to download only Facebook Messenger content so Deselect All and than mark Messages box (1). Mark type of file you want to download, choose JSON type and clik Create File(3).

Step 3

Once your chats are ready (this process can take til 24h) you get notification (3) on your Facebook Page and you click on Available Copies and you can download (3) your Facebook Messenger Chat
